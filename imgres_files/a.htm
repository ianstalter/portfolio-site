Social Media Bootcamp – For Pastors and Church leaders

You might be well equiped in the Social Media world, but do you know people who need some help?
You might know how to keep up with what is going on, but does the pastor or staff at your church?
My company, JoPaProductions is offering specially formulated and specially priced event for church leaders and pastors to learn Social Media.

This Falls event will be Thursday, December 10. Space is limited so register now at
http://www.jopaproductions.com/events/bootcamp

Keep hearing about Facebook, Twitter, Youtube and blogs.
Do you feel out of touch when it comes to Social Networking?
We know it can be hard to navigate this strange new world.
We know it can be embarrasing and intimidating to admidt that you don’t know what to do.

In our one-day Social Media Boot Camps, veteran pastors and social mediaistas, Doug Pagitt and Tony Jones will guide you through the basics of the most popular social media services, including blogging, Facebook, and Twitter. You will learn how, in just three 30-minute periods each week, to have a social media presense that fits you.

We know that no one wants to add more work to their life by “updating their status”. But, we also know that when social media is done intentionally and correctly it can become a life giving and beneficial way to connect with people, learn and stay up to date.

This seminar will also help ministry leaders make the most of Social media for their ministries.
Imagine getting real-time feedback from your parishoners as you prepare your sermon, spreading the word about a church outreach program without buying a newspaper ad, or posting a YouTube welcome to new neighbors in your community.

All of this is possible, and it doesn’t have to be intimidating. In fact, we’ll make it fun!

The Pastors’ Social Media Bootcamp costs $95 and includes lunch. All you need to bring is yourself and your laptop (borrow one if you have to!). We meet from 9am till 4pm at Solomon’s Porch in South Minneapolis. Online registration is a breeze, and you have three dates to choose from (each Boot Camp is limited to 50 registrants)
